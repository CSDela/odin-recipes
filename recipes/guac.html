Guacamole

Let's make some bomb-ass guacamole son

Yo for real this is gonna be lit. Lets get started with some-

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 Ripe Avocados
  • 1/4 Red Onion
  • 2-3 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1/4 Cup of Cilantro (fresh preferred)
  • 1 Jalapeno
  • Pinch of Coarse Salt
  • Pinch of Pepper
  • *OPTIONAL* Pinch of cumin

DIRECTIONS

  1. Crush Garlic. Remove skin/s
  2. Mince garlic (or alternatively add salt and continue crushing into a fine paste). Add to bowl.
  3. Cut open avocados, score, then add to bowl. Mash to desired consistency.
  4. Dice onion, then add to bowl.
  5. Roughly chop cilantro and mince jalapeno. Add to bowl.
  6. Mix all ingredients and add salt, pepper, and desired spices to taste.
