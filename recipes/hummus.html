Hummus

Let's make hummus. Never turns out the same lfg.

I just throw the ingredients together without really measuring so yanno. Just wing it and it'll probably turn out fine.

INGREDIENTS

  • Chickpeas (Canned or dried)
  • Tahini
  • Lemon
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil (Extra Virgin preferred)
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Pepper

DIRECTIONS

  1. Add prepared chickpeas (i.e. rinsed in can or soaked from dry) to food processor/blender/etc. Blend until no large chunks.
  2. Crush, then roughly chop garlic. Add and blend.
  3. Add spoonful of tahini.
  4. Add spices and lemon. Blend until smooth.
  5. If having trouble blending, add olive oil, then blend. Otherwise pour mixture into desired container.
  6. Add olive oil to taste and top off with additional for garnish.
